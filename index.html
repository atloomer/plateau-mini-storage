Here at Plateau Mini Storage, we strive to provide an environment that is clean and safe, while being highly competitive and price conscious. Plateau Mini Storage is locally owned and owner operated: our focus is to provide our customers with a wide variety of options, including indoor and outdoor RV storage. We also offer custom units for small contractors, as well as a large variety of standard mini storage units. We can fit all your needs, whether it be for a short or a long time. Feel free to call or email us with your needs and come see us today.
